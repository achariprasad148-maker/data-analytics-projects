# Omnichannel Retail Sales & Inventory Analysis

## Project Overview
This project develops an end-to-end data analytics pipeline and interactive Power BI dashboard for omnichannel retail sales analysis. The dashboard unifies physical and digital retail data to track sales trends, forecast inventory requirements, and identify top-performing product categories.

## Key Findings
- Total sales achieved: **$2.26M** across all regions and categories
- Consumer segment contributes **50.76%** of total sales ($1.15M)
- Q4 accounts for **50.76%** of annual sales (holiday season peak)
- Saturday generates the **highest revenue** among all days of the week
- Technology category leads in revenue contribution

## Business Recommendations
1. Launch targeted marketing campaigns to the Consumer segment
2. Run Q4 holiday promotions starting October to maximize seasonal spike
3. Increase staff and inventory on Saturdays to handle peak demand
4. Allocate more marketing budget to Technology category
5. Develop loyalty programs for Corporate segment
6. Test promotion strategies in Q1, Q2, Q3 to balance sales distribution
